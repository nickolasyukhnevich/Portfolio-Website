Case Study

Automotive Products Landing Page

A product-focused landing page built for clarity, comparison, and conversion.

The goal of this landing page was to present automotive products in a way that’s simple to scan, easy to compare, and clear about benefits. I focused on a strong hero, clear product cards, and trust elements like reviews.

Project Details

  • Role: Front-End Web Developer
  • Tools: Figma, HTML, CSS
  • Focus: Visual hierarchy, product storytelling

Final Landing Page

High-fidelity layout showing hero, product grid, and supporting content.

Process & Progress

How the layout evolved from loose wireframe to polished interface.

01 — Low-Fidelity Layout

I started with a blocky wireframe to lock in the main sections: hero, product grid, benefits, and CTA. This stage was about structure, not styling.

02 — Product Card Design

Next, I refined product cards with clear titles, specs, and primary actions. Hierarchy ensured the product name and key benefit read first.

03 — Visual Details & Trust

Finally, I layered in imagery, color, and trust badges. Reviews and small visual accents help the page feel more credible and easier to scan.
